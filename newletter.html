The Latest

 There's something new uncorked in Munds Park, and it's pouring heart, flavor, and hometown charm.

 Local resident Deanne Martin has turned a longtime dream into a reality with the opening of Wine Tasting & More, a cozy, welcoming spot built around good wine, good food, and even better company. A full time Munds Park local sinse 2019, Deanne fell in love with the spirit of this mountain community, and now she's pouring that love into every glass.

 "I've always enjoyed entertaining and bringing people together," Deanne says. "Creating a place where neighbors can gather, relax, laugh, and enjoy amazing wine just felt like the right next chapter."

 At Wine Tasting & More, you'll find a carefully curated selection of Arizona wines, paired with charcuterie boards, fondues, caprese skewers, and seasonal small plates. When the snow starts to fall, you can cozy up with homemade chili, hearty soups, and toasted paninis, all thoughtfully matched with wines from Arizona and around the world. Backing Deanne is a team that makes the experience shine. Annette Esposito and Bev Poole bring warmth and friendly expertise to every pour, making sure guests feel right at home the moment they step through the door.
