What We're About

Welcome to Wine Tasting and More, your cozy corner for great wine, good company, and unforgettable flavors right here in the heart of Munds Park, Arizona. We believe that wine is best enjoyed when paired with great food and even better company. That's why we offer a carefully curated selection of wines and beers alongside delicious accompaniments — from beautifully crafted charcuterie boards and Caprese skewers to small cheese boards and rotating seasonal specials. And if you're visiting on the weekend, be sure to treat yourself to one of our refreshing sangrias or fan-favorite wine slushes. Whether you're a Munds Park local, a weekend getaway traveler, or simply passing through on the scenic route between Flagstaff and Sedona, our doors are open and your glass is waiting. We've created a warm, laid-back atmosphere where every guest feels right at home — whether it's your first visit or your fiftieth. Come as you are. Stay a while. Let us pour you something special.
